Alhaja Khairat Olubukola

Founder & Creative Director

With an innate passion for fashion and a heart dedicated to making people's special days even more memorable, Khaaurat Olubukola transformed her childhood fascination with fabrics into a thriving global business.

"My greatest joy comes from seeing the confidence and happiness our fabrics bring to people during their most important celebrations. Quality should be accessible to everyone."

Born into a family of textile merchants, Khaaurat learned the art of fabric selection from her mother at a young age. Exclusive Textile Hub - now serving elite clients across continents while staying true to its roots of offering premium quality at accessible prices.

Today, with flagship stores in both Abuja and Lagos, Khaaurat continues her hands-on approach, personally curating collections and mentoring the next generation of textile experts who share her vision for excellence.
